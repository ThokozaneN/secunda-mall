Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about leasing at Secunda Mall

What are the typical lease terms at Secunda Mall?

Standard lease terms range from 3 to 5 years for retail units, with options for renewal. Shorter terms may be available for kiosks or seasonal businesses. All leases include standard escalation clauses and are subject to credit approval.

Are there any incentives for new tenants?

Yes, we offer various incentives including rental assistance during the fit-out period, marketing support for grand openings, and in some cases, contribution toward tenant installation costs. These are negotiated on a case-by-case basis depending on the business type and lease term.

What are the operating hours and trading restrictions?

Secunda Mall is open Monday to Sunday, with extended hours during peak seasons. Tenants are expected to maintain consistent trading hours. Specific restrictions may apply to certain product categories to maintain the mall's retail mix and brand standards.

Is there support for store design and fit-out?

Yes, our management team provides guidelines and approved contractors for store fit-outs. We offer design consultation services and can connect you with experienced retail architects and contractors familiar with our mall specifications.

What marketing support does the mall provide?

Tenants benefit from regular mall-wide marketing campaigns, digital marketing, social media promotion, seasonal events, and inclusion in the mall directory and website. Additional co-operative marketing opportunities are available throughout the year.

Have more questions? Our leasing team is ready to assist you.
