About Us

My Mall. My Space.

Secunda Mall is the first, and only, fully-realised regional shopping experience for the residents of Secunda and the surrounding areas.

Officially open since 23 October 2013, Secunda Mall offers customers a first class shopping, leisure and dining experience suitable for the whole family. With over 90 stores including major anchor tenants Checkers Hyper, Edgars, Game, Pick n Pay and Woolworths, plus 6 state-of-the-art Ster Kinekor cinemas, of which two are 3D, Secunda Mall has cemented itself as the leading shopping and leisure experience in the region.

The shopping mix includes an array of major fashion, electronics, outdoor, home ware, health and beauty stores. But it's more than just a Mall - Secunda Mall is a meeting place for the community of Secunda, a place where families and friends can gather to shop, socialise and play.

Facilities & Services

Accessibility

Wheelchair friendly with elevators to all levels. Disabled restrooms on both levels. Wheelchairs available at Security.

Located between Nedbank & Clicks (Lower) and Markham & Totalsports (Upper). Includes family & baby changing rooms.

Guide Dogs

Guide dog friendly environment. Other pets are not permitted.

Parking

2,435 free parking bays. Moms & Tots and Disabled bays at all entrances.
