Facilities & Services

Secunda Mall is committed to providing an accessible, comfortable, and convenient shopping experience for all visitors.

Wheelchair Friendly

Secunda Mall is a wheelchair friendly shopping centre. All levels are easily accessible with the use of elevators.

  • Disabled restroom facilities available on both levels
  • Disabled parking bays near all entrances
  • Wheelchairs available at Security

Restroom Facilities

Clean, well-maintained restrooms with family-friendly amenities.

  • Located between Nedbank & Clicks (lower level)
  • Located between Markham & Totalsports (upper level)
  • Baby changing rooms and family restrooms

Family Friendly

Designed with families in mind for a comfortable shopping experience.

  • Moms and Tots Parking Bays at each entrance
  • Parent & child facilities in restrooms
  • Baby changing stations at multiple locations

Electronic Directory

State-of-the-art Electronic Directory Boards at all main entrances.

  • Interactive digital maps
  • Detailed store information
  • Easy navigation for all visitors

Guide Dog Friendly

Welcoming environment for visitors with guide dogs.

  • Guide dogs are welcome in the Centre
  • Assistance animals permitted
  • Other pets are not permitted

Parking

Ample, convenient, and free parking for all visitors.

  • 2,435 parking bays available
  • All parking is free of charge
  • Bike parking at Entrances 1, 3, and 4
